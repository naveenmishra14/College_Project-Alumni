Why SDBC-Indore?

Indore is a manufacturing and operations heavy city and SDBC Indore has its specialization in the same which helps make the students industry ready also Indore has special SEZs for Engineering, manufacturing & IT industries which helps in job influx. This is inception batch which is trained to learn the hardships and build an organization from scratch.

As pioneers of this institution the focus is on setting bench mark on performance and to provide a strong foundation in course content and management techniques.

Professional counseling for Assessment of students to identify their core competence along with emphasis on strategic levelfunctioning with a view to produce business leaders and captains of the industry.
